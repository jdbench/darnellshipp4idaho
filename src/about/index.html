Work Life

Where I Stand

I'm a constitutional conservative. My support lies with the right to life, protecting Idaho family values, and parental rights. It is my belief that in regards to education, money should follow the child and Common Core should be done away with.

Why I'm Running

A large reason that I am running for office is my concern surrounding government outreach, specifically at the federal level. Vaccine mandates are a threat; they force employees throughout Idaho to choose between bodily autonomy and a paycheck.

Family Life
